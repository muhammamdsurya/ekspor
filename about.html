About Us

Description of Edgy Craft

What is Edgy Craft?

Eden garden craft is one of the leading ceramic craft companies in Indonesia. In the handicraft industry we not only sell products but also sell art itself both for function needs and home interiors. As our company name eden garden sounds like heaven, right we will decorate every interior of your home like eden paradise and make your home more beautiful

Why we call it Edgy Craft because it's stand for edgy style which is a different style from the others. Our company presents an edgy style in the art itself and juxtaposed with a futuristic modern style as per your need for beauty.

What We do?

Our company goals is to market products worldwide and offer customer satisfaction in accordance with demand.

We continue to develop our art products and are sustainable. also, beside that help local Indonesian artists who have high artistic value and futuristic be glimpsed in the international market.
